What is Comfort Crate?

Feel the love and share the bliss as you pick through the variety of self-care packages Comfort Crate curated just for you.

Feeling a bit hungry? Try our snacks set which contains a variety of goods perfect for that sweet tooth. Want to explore your creativity? Consider purchasing our stationery package and let your passion and ideas flow. Or maybe, you just want to unwind and take a break from the tiring reality? Then try our relaxation set and allow it to aid you in releasing stress and negativity from your body.
If you want a little bit of everything, our customizable set is designed exactly just for that. We also offer personalized add-ons such as polaroid photos, playlist cards, handcrafted beaded bracelets, and handcrafted mini dried flower bouquets to make your package extra special (even more so if you wish to give it to a special someone).

Comfort Crate is inclusive to the general public and is perfect for individuals who aim to foster the importance of self-care within themselves and others around them.

Vision Statement

To spread happiness and relaxation throughout the world by embodying a healthy mentality.
Mission Statement

The mission of Comfort Crate is to promote the significance of self-care, where individuals can impose an effort in lessening the pressure of everyday life by selecting bundles filled with stress relieving and soothing products.
Value Proposition/USP

Our store, Comfort Crate, seeks to aid individuals interested in self-care for themselves and their loved ones through products that can not only alleviate stress and anxiety, but also provide relaxation and happiness. Consequently, unlike other brands that typically only sell fixed or random items, our packages are more adaptable to the customers' demands, as they come in a variety of specified and customizable sets.
History

Our entire process of creation is soaked with the concept of creating whimsical objects with thoughtful craft.
